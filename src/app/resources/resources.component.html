What can you do?

Home

Try to limit the length of your showers to 5 minutes. Keep in mind that baths use significantly more water than showers.

During the winter, the recommended temperature is 68 degrees Fahrenheit. In the summer, try to keep the AC no lower than 72.

Travel

Use Public Transport as much as possible. Transit app for iOS and Android is very useful for determining routes and schedules.

If you can, find a bus/ train route to get to school/work, or organize a carpool with coworkers near you. Using a car to commute 5 days a week all year quickly adds up!

Community

Look into powering your home with renewable energy such as solar panels. The more people participating, the more will feel inclined to participate!
